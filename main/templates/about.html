Our Story

Since 2018, Yoga & Yoga Therapy Center Indore has been dedicated to improving physical health and overall well-being through classical yoga, meditation, pranayama and therapeutic interventions. Under the guidance of Acharya Manish Choubey, we've helped more than 95% of our students meet their wellness goals—and we continue to serve ever more people from all walks of life.

Mission & Vision

Holistic Health: Promote holistic health and inner balance using time-tested yogic practices
Personalized Approach: Offer personalized sessions that adapt to each student's needs—whether they're beginners or experienced practitioners
Community Growth: Foster a supportive community where body, mind and spirit can grow in harmony

What We Offer

All programs are tailored to your unique needs, helping you move safely, breathe more deeply, and feel more centered.

Group & Private Classes

  • Hatha Yoga
  • Vinyasa Flow
  • Therapeutic Yoga
  • Yoga with Chiropractic & Physio Therapy

Specialized Programs

  • Pre- and post-natal conditioning
  • Alignment-focused asana workshops
  • Meditation & kriya sessions
  • Pranayama mastery

Yoga Therapy for Common Ailments

  • Musculoskeletal: knee pain, frozen shoulder, slipped disc, spondylitis
  • Neurological & stress-related: migraine, insomnia, depression
  • Respiratory & systemic: asthma, sinus, PCOD, ankle pain

200 Hours Teachers Training Course

  • Certification Course : Your first step towards being a yoga teacher
  • You will learn the tools to master your physical, mental and spiritual well-being. You will see a marked improvement in your life, relationships and work.
  • A new career avenue opens up for you where you can pass on the knowledge to others and facilitate their wellness journeys. The art of Yoga is something you can teach anywhere, no matter where you are.

300 Hours Teachers Training Course

  • Advanced Certification Course: for a more professional dive into yoga teachers training
  • Prequisite: 200 Hours Teachers Training Course
  • Gain additional knowledge about yoga and become a well rounded professional.

90 Day Yoga Therapy Course

  • Provides an in-depth understanding of yoga as a therapeutic practice, focusing on its application for various physical and mental health conditions
  • Beyond professional training, the yoga therapy course emphasize personal growth and the development of a yogic lifestyle.
  • Learn to apply yoga practices like asanas (postures), pranayama (breathwork), and meditation to address various conditions such as stress, anxiety, back pain, and digestive issues.
